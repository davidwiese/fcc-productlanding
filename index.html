Simple Packs for Simple Hikers

All packs are 100% designed in the USA! All packs are now sewn by our skilled tailoring partners in Vietnam.

Packs are available in Ultra Weave, Gridstop, and Robic Nylon. We have discontinued the use of Dyneema Composite Fabric due to cost and supply chain shortages.

We take full responsibility for manufacturing defects. Reach out to us and we will send you a replacement pack ASAP at no charge to you. Please understand that this pack will not last a lifetime under heavy use. We have tested and used these packs extensively and expect them to last a full thru-hike.
